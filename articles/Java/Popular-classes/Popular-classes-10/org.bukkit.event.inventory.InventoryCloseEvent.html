org.bukkit.event.inventory.InventoryCloseEvent

The org.bukkit.event.inventory.InventoryCloseEvent is a specialized event that is fired when an inventory is closed by a player. This could occur when a player leaves the game, when they switch to a different inventory view, or when they are kicked from the server.
